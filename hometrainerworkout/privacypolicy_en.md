# Privacy statement of Hometrainer Workout

Last updated: March 5, 2026

The Hometrainer Workout developers are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, use, and disclose the information we obtain from users of the application. By using the Hometrainer Workout application, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Statement.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- Third-Party Accounts: You can log in to the application using accounts with other businesses, such as Strava. If you access the Services through Third-Party Accounts, we will collect information such as your name, email address, profile information, and preferences. To control the information we receive from Third-Party Accounts, you must use the privacy controls in your Third-Party Account.
- Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with our application, such as the screen you visit, the features you use and the actions you take.
- Device Information: We collect technical information from the mobile device used to access the Services. This includes device and analytics information.
- Connected Devices: We collect information (including Activity Data) from devices you choose to connect to the application. This could include, power, cadence or heart rate. If we collect health information from these integrations (such as heart rate), we will not sell or use it for advertising or other similar purposes; we do not disclose it to third parties without your prior consent; and we will only use it for the specific purposes described in this Policy.
- Activity Data: We collect Activity Data directly or through devices connected to the application. “Activity Data” is information about the activities you record , such as equipment usage, date, time, power and cadence. Activity Data can also include health data, such as heart rate, if you choose to provide it.

## 2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- Record your Activities: We process your Activity Data and other information to help you record and analyze your performance, and to upload this information to Third-Party Accounts when you choose so.
- To provide and improve our services: We use your information to deliver the services you request and to enhance your experience. This includes analyzing usage patterns, and improving the functionality and performance of our application.
- Aggregated and Anonymized Data: We may aggregate and anonymize the information we collect to create statistical data that does not identify any individual. This data may be used for research, analytics, and other purposes.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties other than the Third Party Accounts you have logged with. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

- Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, please be aware that no security measures are foolproof, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The Hometrainer Workout application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.

## 6. Changes to this Privacy Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Statement on application store page.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Statement or our privacy practices please contact us, as described in the Settings/About of the application.
